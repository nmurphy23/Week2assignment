Information about Summerlin

Summerlin is located on the west side of Las Vegas and is widely known for the world famous Red Rocks, a popular tourist destination and great spot for hiking many different trails.

Top trails to hike in Red Rock

  1. Calico Tanks Trail
  2. Turtlehead Peak Trail
  3. First Creek Canyon Trail to the Grotto
