Information about Henderson

The city of Henderson, Nevada is about 16 miles southeast of the Las Vegas strip and is the second largest city in Nevada. This side of the city is very family friendly and has a ton of recreational activities.

Recreational activities near Henderson

  1. Visiting Lake Mead for a fun day of boating on the lake
  2. Heading further east and visiting the icon Hoover Dam
  3. Shopping at the District at Green Valley Ranch
