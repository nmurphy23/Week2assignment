Information about the Las Vegas Strip

The Las Vegas Strip runs about 4.2 miles long and is one of the most photographed and visited boulevards of the world. The official name of the strip is Las Vegas Boulevard and is home to many attractions and exhibits.

Things to do and see on the Las Vegas Strip

  1. Watch the Fountains waterwork show at the Bellagio
  2. Visit the High Roller, the second highest ferris wheel in the world
  3. Visit the world famous M&M's and Hershey store
